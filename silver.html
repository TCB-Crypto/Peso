Philippine Silver Coins Reference Guide

Spanish-Philippines Era (Pre-1898)

  • 1/2 Real – Silver (.900+)
  • 1 Real – Silver (.900+)
  • 2 Reales – Silver (.900+)
  • 4 Reales – Silver (.900+)
  • 8 Reales (Spanish Dollar) – Silver (.900+)

American-Philippines Era (1903–1945)

  • 1 Peso (1903–1912) – 90% Silver
  • 50 Centavos (1903–1912) – 90% Silver
  • 20 Centavos (1903–1912) – 90% Silver
  • 10 Centavos (1903–1912) – 90% Silver
  • 50 Centavos (1920s–1945) – 75% or 65% Silver
  • 20 Centavos (1920s–1945) – 75% or 65% Silver
  • 10 Centavos (1920s–1945) – 75% or 65% Silver

Republic of the Philippines (1946–1966)

  • MacArthur Commemorative Peso (1964) – 90% Silver

Modern Commemoratives (1970s–Present)

  • Various Marcos-era commemoratives – Mixed Silver Content
  • Selected Central Bank Proof Sets – Silver content varies
